Why We Do What We Do

Japan is one of the world's most attractive consumer markets — but also one of the hardest to enter. Language barriers, complex regulations, unique consumer expectations, and a crowded retail landscape make it challenging for international manufacturers to succeed alone.

At Mosca Alegre, we exist to solve exactly this problem. With deep expertise in Japanese crowdfunding platforms, regulatory compliance, and e-commerce, we serve as your trusted local partner — handling every aspect of market entry so you can focus on creating great products.

Our mission is simple: to connect innovative global products with Japanese consumers who will love them.
