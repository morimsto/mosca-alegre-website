How We Bring Your Product to Japan

Our Process

Five Steps to the Japanese Market

1

Product Evaluation

We assess your product's fit for the Japanese market — including pricing, competition, regulatory requirements (PSE, TELEC, etc.), and consumer demand. Only products with genuine potential move forward.

2

Exclusive Agreement

We sign an exclusive distribution agreement for the Japanese market, ensuring your brand is protected and represented by a single, dedicated partner.

3

Crowdfunding Campaign

We launch your product on Japan's leading crowdfunding platforms — Makuake, machi-ya, or GREEN FUNDING. This validates market demand, generates revenue, and builds an initial customer base before full-scale distribution.

4

E-Commerce Rollout

After a successful crowdfunding campaign, we expand to major Japanese e-commerce channels including Amazon Japan and Rakuten, creating sustainable long-term sales.

5

Ongoing Support

We handle ongoing operations — inventory management, customer support in Japanese, marketing, and reorder management — so you can focus on what you do best: making great products.

What We Handle

Everything You Need, Covered

📋 Regulatory Compliance

We navigate Japan's complex regulations, including PSE (electrical safety), TELEC (radio), and food import requirements, ensuring your product is fully compliant.

🚚 Logistics & Customs

From international shipping to customs clearance and domestic warehousing, we handle the entire supply chain within Japan.

🌏 Japanese Localization

Product descriptions, manuals, marketing materials, and customer communications — all professionally translated and culturally adapted for the Japanese market.

📣 Marketing & PR

Press releases, social media campaigns, influencer outreach, and advertising on Japanese platforms to maximize visibility and sales.
